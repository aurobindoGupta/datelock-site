# DateLock

**Let customers choose a delivery date at checkout — and make sure it always lands on the order, without ever breaking checkout.**

DateLock is a Shopify app for date-sensitive stores — bakeries, florists, meal kits, fresh food, and local delivery. Customers pick a delivery or pickup date, DateLock requires one before checkout, and the chosen date is saved to the order and shown on the order page, thank-you page, and confirmation email.
